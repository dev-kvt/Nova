# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Nova a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Be Respectful

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community

### Be Collaborative

- Work together toward common goals
- Share knowledge and resources
- Give and gracefully accept feedback
- Help others learn and grow

### Be Professional

- Maintain professional behavior
- Respect others' time and contributions
- Keep discussions constructive
- Stay focused on the work

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
